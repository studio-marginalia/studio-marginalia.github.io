Is This For You?

You're in the right place if:

  • You've finished a draft but aren't sure if the story actually works
  • You're stuck and need direction for your next revision
  • Your draft is complete but needs expert diagnosis on what's working and what could be improved
  • You need deep structural analysis before hitting the publish button
  • You have a world, characters, and a vision but can't translate it into a working draft
  • You're drowning in outlines and notes and need someone to synthesize it into structure you can follow


This isn't for you if:

  • Your manuscript isn't readable yet (I need to follow the story, not decode it)
  • You're looking for line editing, grammar fixes, or copyediting (I diagnose story problems, not sentence problems)
  • You want someone to fix your book for you (I show you what's broken and how to fix it, but you do the work)
  • You're not ready for honest feedback (I tell you what's not working and what you can do better, and that might sting)

Your manuscript should be readable: clear grammar and sentence structure. I don't expect perfection, just a draft polished enough to follow. If you're still on a 'vomit draft,' take another pass to clean it up so your book feels finished, complete, and ready to stand on its own.
