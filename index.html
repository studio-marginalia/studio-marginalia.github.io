Is This For You?

You're in the right place if:

  • You've finished a draft but aren't sure if the story works
  • You're stuck and need clear direction for your next revision
  • You want a deep, honest assessment before publishing


This isn't for you if:

  • Your draft isn't ready for another pair of eyes
  • You're looking for line editing, copyediting, or grammar fixes
  • You're not ready for honest, direct feedback

Your draft doesn't need to be perfect but it should be clear enough to read and follow.
