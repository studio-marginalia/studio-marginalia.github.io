Is This For You?

You're in the right place if:

  • You've finished a draft but aren't sure if the story works
  • You're stuck and need clear direction for your next revision
  • You want a deep, honest assessment before publishing


This might not be the fit if:

  • Your draft isn't ready for another pair of eyes
  • You're looking for line editing, copyediting, or grammar fixes
  • You're not ready for honest, direct feedback

Your draft doesn't need to be perfect but it should be clear enough to read and follow.

The Process

From your first enquiry to receiving your feedback, here's exactly what you can expect.

1

Choose Your Service

Get in touch through the contact form or email info.studiomarginalia@gmail.com. Tell me about your manuscript, where you are in the writing process, and whether you're interested in Beta Reading or a Manuscript Critique.

2

Optional Sample Critique

If you'd like to see how I work before committing, send me your first 2,000 words. I'll return honest feedback within 48 hours so you can decide if we're the right fit.

3

Book Your Project

Once you're ready to move forward, I'll send over a proposal that includes the contract and invoice. To secure your booking, simply review and sign the contract, pay the 50% deposit, and send your manuscript (Google Docs or Word).

4

The Review Begins

Your manuscript is now in my hands. I'll confirm your delivery date before I begin, and you're always welcome to reach out for an update while I'm working.

Beta Reading: Delivered within 15 business days.
Manuscript Critique: Delivered within 30 business days.

5

Final Payment

Once your feedback is complete, I'll let you know it's ready and request the remaining 50% balance.

6

Receive Your Feedback

As soon as final payment is received, I'll email your completed feedback.

Beta Reading: Annotated manuscript + Beta Reading Report.
Manuscript Critique: Annotated manuscript + Comprehensive Critique Letter.

After Delivery

Manuscript Critique

  • • One follow-up call (no time limit) to discuss your feedback in depth.

Beta Reading
