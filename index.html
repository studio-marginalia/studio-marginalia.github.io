Is This For You?

You're in the right place if:

  • You've finished a draft but aren't sure if the story actually works
  • You're stuck and need direction for your next revision
  • You’re still finding your story and looking for guidance on how to proceed
  • You've gotten feedback before but it was too vague to be useful
  • You've studied the craft but can't figure out how to apply it to YOUR story
  • You're preparing to self-publish or query agents and want to ensure your book is strong first


This isn't for you if:

  • Your manuscript is still a rough first draft that needs basic editing before it's readable (clean it up first, then come back!)
  • Your manuscript needs grammar or copyediting (I focus on story, not sentences)
  • You want someone to rewrite your book for you (I give direction; you do the revisions)
  • You're not open to honest feedback about what needs improvement

Your manuscript should be readable: clear grammar and sentence structure. I don't expect perfection, just a draft polished enough to follow. If you're still on a 'vomit draft,' take another pass to clean it up so your book feels finished, complete, and ready to stand on its own.
