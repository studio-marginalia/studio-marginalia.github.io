Is This For You?

You're in the right place if:

  • You've finished a draft but aren't sure if the story works
  • You're stuck and need clear direction for your next revision
  • You want a deep, honest assessment before publishing


This might not be the fit if:

  • Your draft isn't ready for another pair of eyes
  • You're looking for line editing, copyediting, or grammar fixes
  • You're not ready for honest, direct feedback

Your draft doesn't need to be perfect but it should be clear enough to read and follow.

The Process

1

You Reach Out

Contact me through the form on my website or email info.studiomarginalia@gmail.com. Tell me about your manuscript and what you're looking for.

2

Optional Sample Read

Send me your first 2,000 words. I'll give you honest feedback within 48 hours so you can judge if we're a good fit.

3

We Agree to Work Together

If the sample felt right (or you skip it), I send you a proposal with all the details. Submit your 50% deposit and share your manuscript via Google Docs or Word. Payment via PayPal Xoom or Western Union.

4

The Read Begins

I give your manuscript my full attention, commenting as I go, and write a detailed report analyzing what worked, what didn't, and why. Delivered within 25 business days.

5

Request Final Payment

I notify you that your feedback is ready and request the remaining 50% payment.

6

Receive Your Feedback

Once final payment is received, I send you the annotated manuscript and your detailed Reader Report.

7

Follow-Up Call

We schedule a follow-up call (no time limit) to discuss your feedback, clarify questions, and brainstorm revision approaches.

8

60-Day Revision Support

You have 60 days of unlimited email support for revision questions. I'll respond within 72 hours.
