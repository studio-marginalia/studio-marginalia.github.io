Beta Reading

Reader-perspective feedback that shows you where your story hooks, drags, and falls flat

Before you begin your next round of revisions, it's worth knowing how your story lands with a fresh set of eyes.

A beta read tests the reading experience before your manuscript reaches the audience you're writing it for. I read your story as an engaged first-time reader, documenting my thoughts and reactions as they naturally unfold. Rather than analysing every storytelling decision, I focus on the experience of reading the manuscript itself: where I became invested, where my attention drifted, what surprised me, and what stayed with me after the final page.

Who This Service Is For

A beta read is ideal if:

  • You've completed a draft and want an honest reader-first perspective
  • You want to know whether your story delivers on its promise before investing in deeper editorial work
  • You're looking for genuine reader reactions rather than an editorial critique
  • You're preparing to query, self-publish, or move on to professional editing
What You Receive

In-line Comments

For beta reading, the comments are the experience. They capture my real-time reactions as I read: excitement, confusion, curiosity, predictions, and questions. You'll see exactly how individual scenes and story beats land with a reader.

Detailed Reader Report

After finishing your manuscript, you'll receive a concise report covering my overall reading experience, including:

  • Plot

    Whether the story remained engaging, coherent, and satisfying from beginning to end.

  • Characters

    Which characters I connected with most, whose motivations felt believable, and who left the strongest impression.

  • Setting/Worldbuilding

    Whether the world felt immersive, consistent, and easy to visualise.

  • Pacing

    Where the story maintained momentum, where it slowed, and where my attention began to drift.

  • Emotional Impact

    Which moments resonated most strongly, what surprised me, and whether the emotional beats landed as intended.

  • Overall Reader Experience

    My overall impressions after reaching the final page, including what stayed with me and what readers are most likely to remember.

  • Genre & Reader Expectations

    Whether the manuscript delivered the experience I expected from its genre and who I believe it will resonate with most.

Before You Submit

If there are particular questions you'd like answered while I read, you're welcome to include them with your manuscript. Whether you're concerned about a character arc, a subplot, your ending, or anything else, I'll keep those questions in mind throughout the read.

What This Service Doesn't Include

Beta Reading is designed to evaluate the reading experience, not provide editorial analysis. As such, this service does not include:

  • Developmental editing
  • Line editing or copyediting
  • Proofreading for grammar, spelling, or punctuation
  • Detailed craft analysis or a structured revision plan.

If you're looking for in-depth editorial guidance and actionable revision advice, the Manuscript Critique is likely the better fit.

Communication

Beta Reading is an email-only service.

Once your beta read is complete, you'll receive your annotated manuscript and Beta Reading Report by email. If you have any questions about the report afterwards, you're welcome to send one follow-up email for clarification.

Questions?

If you’re unsure whether this service is the right fit for your manuscript’s current stage, feel free to get in touch. I’m happy to help you figure out what kind of feedback would best support your revision goals.
