Skilled Beta Reading

$80 per 10,000 words


Feedback delivered within 25 business days.

This service offers honest reader-focused feedback on your manuscript. I read as your stand-in audience, leave inline comments with my real-time reactions, and provide a detailed letter breaking down what worked, what fell short and why, and actionable suggestions to strengthen your story.

What You Receive

In-line Comments

Light in-line comments giving you an insider view into my thoughts and emotions as I read your story. You'll see my real-time reactions (predictions, questions, excitement, frustration, confusion) across various story elements (character actions, plot direction, internal monologues, etc).

Detailed Reader Report

The Reader Report is a letter focusing on:

  • Plot

    If the story moves logically, has sufficient conflict, tension, and lands emotionally.

  • Characters

    Whether they feel believable, their motivations make sense, if they serve the story, and whether I connected with them.

  • Setting/Worldbuilding

    If the world feels immersive, consistent, and enhances (or detracts from) the story.

  • Theme

    Whether the story's message or central idea comes through clearly and feels earned.

  • Pacing

    How quickly or slowly the story moves and whether it keeps me engaged or loses momentum.

  • Readability and Flow

    How smoothly the prose reads and whether scenes transition naturally.

  • Overall Reader Experience

    How it all comes together and what stays with me as a complete story.

My feedback on each element is broken down into two sections:

What's Working

What resonates with me as a reader and why you managed to pull it off so well.

Notes on Improvement

Constructive feedback on what isn't working, why it affects the reading experience, and how you can strengthen it.

Communication

This is a hybrid email-call service designed to be flexible and low-pressure. Email is my primary channel, but I recommend a follow-up call after your feedback to maximize the value you gain.

I do not use AI at any point in my service: to read your manuscript, write your report, or assist in writing the report. If you suspect AI use, you are well within your rights to request proof of actual human work, which will be provided. Studio Marginalia is built for humans, by a human.

Payments

All services and payments will be agreed upon in advance and paid in USD. Payments are processed through PayPal Xoom or Western Union.

Payment Structure

All services are confirmed with a 50% deposit to secure the booking and begin the project. The remaining 50% balance is due upon completion of the service and before delivery of final materials (your Reader Report and annotated manuscript). Final deliverables are released once the remaining balance has been received.

Timeline
